Phonemes in Hýyban each have a weight of either light or heavy. When forming words, a syllable will bind to the prior syllables if they collectively have less than two heavy phonemes.When the collective weight of the syllables becomes two or more, the word ends and a new word can begin.

The consonant cluster /nn/ is realized as [mn].
